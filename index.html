About NayePankh Foundation

📚

Education First

We believe every child deserves access to quality education regardless of their economic background. Our programs focus on foundational literacy, numeracy, and life skills training.

🤝

Community Driven

Our volunteers are the backbone of NayePankh. Together, we create supportive learning environments and mentorship programs that transform lives and inspire hope.

🎯

Sustainable Impact

We focus on creating long-term sustainable solutions. Our programs are designed to empower students to become agents of change in their own communities.

Why Volunteer With Us?

  • Make a direct impact on a child's future
  • Develop leadership and mentoring skills
  • Build a network of like-minded changemakers
  • Gain valuable work experience
  • Be part of a supportive volunteer community
